KINGSTON BREEDING

Kingston Breeding is a family owned breeding service, specializing in the breeding of healthy, happy, and well-trained, Pocket Bullies.

At Kingston Breeding, we pride ourselves on our large selction of true blood Pocket Bullies. We put extra care into our breeding process, ensuring that our Pocket Bullies are the product of pure, healthy bloodlines, of well documented Bullies. 

We feed out Bullies a special blend of nutrious dog food, to ensure that our Pocket Bullies grow up to be strong, vigorous, adults, suitable for both protecting your home, and cuddling with your children.

We breed our mama Pocket Bully only once a season, making sure to care for her properly, all the while breeding an elite and exclusive line of Pocket Bullies.
